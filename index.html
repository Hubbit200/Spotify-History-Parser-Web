Spotify History Analyzer

Click on Select File above, and select the "StreamingHistory0.json" file from your Spotify data download.

If you have not yet downloaded it:
1. Go to the Spotify website and log in
2. Go to account settings, then privacy options
3. Follow the steps near the bottom of the page to download your data. It usually takes around 2-4 days.

Internet Explorer and other outdated browsers are not supported.
